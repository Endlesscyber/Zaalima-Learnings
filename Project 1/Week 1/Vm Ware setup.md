## Step 1: Download VMware

Go to the official VMware website and download **VMware Workstation Player** — it's free for personal use.

- Visit **vmware.com** → Products → Workstation Player
- Click **Download for Free** and choose your OS (Windows or Linux)
- Save the installer file to your desktop or Downloads folder


---

## Step 2: Install VMware

Run the installer you just downloaded and follow the on-screen steps.

- Double-click the installer file to open it
- Click **Next**, accept the license agreement, and click **Next** again
- Keep the default settings and click **Install**
- Once done, click **Finish** and restart your computer if prompted

---

## Step 3: Create a New Virtual Machine

Open VMware Player and start creating your virtual machine (VM).

- Open **VMware Workstation Player** from the Start menu
- Click **"Create a New Virtual Machine"**
- A setup wizard will appear — follow along step by step

---

## Step 4: Select Your ISO File

An ISO file is a digital copy of an operating system disc (like Windows or Linux).

- Choose **"Installer disc image file (ISO)"**
- Click **Browse** and locate your downloaded ISO file
- Click **Next** to continue


---

## Step 5: Allocate RAM and Storage

Tell VMware how much of your computer's resources to share with the virtual machine.

- **RAM:** Set at least **2048 MB (2 GB)** — 4096 MB is better if you can spare it
- **Storage:** Allocate at least **20 GB** of disk space for the VM
- Leave other settings at their defaults for now


---

## Step 6: Review and Finish

You'll see a summary of your VM settings before anything is created.

- Review the summary screen — check the RAM, storage, and ISO path
- Click **Finish** to create the virtual machine
- VMware will now build the VM — this takes just a few seconds

---

## Step 7: Power On and Install the OS

Your VM is ready — now boot it up and install the operating system from the ISO.

- Select your new VM from the list and click **"Play virtual machine"**
- The OS installation will start automatically from the ISO
- Follow the installer steps just like you would on a real computer
- Once done, your virtual machine is fully set up and ready to use!

---

##  You're All Set!

Your virtual machine is now running inside your computer. It's completely isolated — anything you do inside it won't affect your main system.

---

| Setting | Minimum | Recommended |

| Storage | 20 GB | 40 GB+ |
